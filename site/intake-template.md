# Contact / Intake Handling Template

Use this when the site is deployed and leads start arriving.

## Lead capture fields
- Name
- Business
- Email
- Website
- Best contact channel
- Biggest follow-up problem right now
- Primary channel in scope (email / DMs / other)
- What would a win look like in 14 days?

## Manual handling rule
Until a real form backend is connected, treat inbound interest as manual-review only.

## Next action after receiving a lead
1. add the lead to the outreach tracker or pilot-status sheet
2. send a short acknowledgment
3. decide whether to book discovery or ask one qualifying question first
