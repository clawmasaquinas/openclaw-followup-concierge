# Intake Modes

Default the pilot to a **scoped intake path**, not broad inbox access.

## Preferred intake modes
### 1. Forwarded lead emails
Client forwards only relevant lead emails to the pilot workflow.

Best for:
- fast setup
- lower trust barrier
- narrow scope

### 2. Shared lead inbox
Pilot covers a dedicated inbox like:
- `leads@company.com`
- `sales@company.com`
- `hello@company.com`

Best for:
- teams with a clear lead intake mailbox
- clean separation from personal/work email

### 3. CRM / form / webhook feed
Lead data arrives from a form, CRM, or another structured intake source.

Best for:
- cleaner automation
- less email dependence
- more structured lead handling

### 4. Read-only inbox assist
Use only when needed.
Read-only or narrowly scoped access for triage/drafting support.

Best for:
- clients who already live in one inbox workflow
- cases where forwarding/shared inbox is not practical

## Avoid by default
### Full inbox access
This should be the exception, not the standard pilot path.

Why:
- higher privacy/trust barrier
- broader scope than needed
- harder sales story
- harder onboarding/offboarding

## Default recommendation
For most first pilots:
- start with **forwarded leads** or a **shared lead inbox**
- keep the workflow narrow
- keep human review in the loop
